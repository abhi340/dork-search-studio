About

Dork Search Studio helps you find exactly what you're looking for online by building precise search queries — instead of typing a few words and scrolling through pages of noise.

It uses well-known search-engine operators (often called "Google dorking"): things like site:, filetype:, intitle:, exact phrases, exclusions and date filters. These are public features of search engines — this site just makes them easy to use.

Built for every level

New to this? The step-by-step builder asks one friendly question at a time and assembles the query for you. Already a power user? You'll get a complete, copy-ready dork at the end, and the Templates page gives you instant recipes. Want to actually understand it? The Learn page explains every operator.

How it works

Everything runs entirely in your browser. There is no server, no account, and no database. When you press a search button, it simply opens the chosen search engine (Google, Bing, or DuckDuckGo) with your query — the same as if you'd typed it yourself.

Use it responsibly

Dorking only surfaces pages that search engines have already indexed and made public. Please use this tool for learning, research, and systems you own or are authorised to test — never to access or misuse private data. See our Terms for details.
