Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Short version: we don't collect anything. Dork Search Studio runs entirely in your browser and has no server, account system, analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

What we store

The only thing saved on your device is your theme preference (light or dark), kept in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device and we cannot see it. Clearing your browser data removes it.

What we don't do

  • No cookies for tracking.
  • No accounts, logins, or personal data collection.
  • No analytics, fingerprinting, or advertising scripts.
  • No third-party scripts at all — every file is served from this site.

Searches

When you click a search button, your query is sent to the search engine you chose (Google, Bing, or DuckDuckGo) by opening it in a new tab — exactly as if you typed the query there yourself. That search is then governed by that engine's privacy policy, not ours. We use no-referrer so the engine isn't told you came from here.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the "last updated" date above will change with it.
